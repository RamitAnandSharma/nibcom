Embeddable Software Sensors for the Smart Home that react to Your Needs

NabComm is building a world where we can easily and safely connect people and all the things that make our lives better. Our software enables Internet of Things (IoT) and machine-to-machine (M2M) network architects and service providers around the world to address security, interoperability, flexibility and scalability from a single IoT solution

About Application

The NabComm Smart Home Solution is as impressive on the inside as it is on the outside. It contains sensors that can estimate energy usage, wirelessly communicate with your smartphone, and accurately sense your location.

Energy Dashboard

The on-board energy metering processor provides accurate real-time energy measurement of any device that is plugged in

Security & Privacy

It’s your home, you should always feel safe and secure. NabbComm handles your data with the utmost respect and protection, and is consistently improving its security methods.

Routine

Imagine coming home to a warm, perfectly lit room without ever touching a thing. No switches, no dials — just instant ambiance that adapts to your preferences. It’s like you’re one with the surroundings, your home catering to whatever makes you most comfortable.

SAVING ENERGY

It takes a lot of electricity to power all your creature comforts. Having more insight into where that energy goes leads to smarter decisions about usage — and sweeter savings on your utility bill — without sacrificing the little things you love.
